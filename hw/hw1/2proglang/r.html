It is a GNU (GNU's Nt Unix!? What's with these recursive acronyms?) project
similar to a language called S (You can do your own investiation if you want to
know more about that).

R provides a wide range of stat and graph techniques. The S language is
considered to be the most popular language for stat research. R is the
open source version.
