Main Creeper Trojan Brain
A Trojan virus is piggy backed on another seemingly harmless program.
Hidden within another program, does whatever it wants without permission
from the user.

The first Trojan was created when John Walker wanted a convenient way
to send a popular game to his friends. He wrote a program called
Prevade, which installed itself with the game he was sending.
Once installed, Prevade would make a copy of the game in any directory
that didn't have it...
The source says there was no malicious intent here...
... and I am still fuzzy on how this made distribution easier.

I think all my friends need a copy of the latest MMORPG in every directory
on their computer. I need Prevade! Or its 2018 equivalent!
